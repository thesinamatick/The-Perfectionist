THE PERFECTIONIST - The lives of a costume store owner, two mobsters, a snuff filmmaker, intertwine on a day of total mayhem.
INFLUENCES:
Pulp Fiction - Inspired much of the dialogue and of course the inclusion of the two witty hitmen. It also inspired the overall dark, morbid humor seen in my story. I want that kind of hip vibe during the more routine worldly scenes where things are normal.

The Texas Chain Saw Massacre - Inspired the level of grotesque, unsettling violence once the story shows its more horror elements. That ghastly aura seen here is something I wanted to harness to leave an unforgettable impression, I also included some callbacks to this film.

8MM - A CRIMINALLY underrated thriller about investigating snuff films. Of course, this was a major factor in writing this story. Snuff films have always intrigued me, they're that devilish crime you rarely hear about. I think snuff films are a goldmine for horror films that haven't had their time to bloom just yet. We've got regular slasher films, psychological films, supernatural horror but I think snuff films would be a neat way to expand the slasher genre to new ventures that haven't been properly explored yet.

Saw - One of the greatest indie films ever in my opinion. That rawness, that sense of hopelessness and cruelty was something that inspired me to write this story.
